“Ensemble” is a fast-paced, character-driven, half-hour, situational dramedy. It highlights the complexity and hilarity of conservatory theatre at the university level.
From full-on lead role take-downs to the pressure cookers of being forced to work together to make a 24-hour live theatre showcase happen, this show explores the nuance and gut-busting irony of the shows within a show
It is scripted! Faster-paced and less self-deprecating than Glee, and less cheesy and more self-aware than High School Musical. No big musical numbers. Think Parks & Rec-type mockumentary style, but with college students, and a stage not big enough for all of them.
